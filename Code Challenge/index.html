Grade Calculator

Use this calculator to find out the grade of a Student pass-mark. This calculator accepts only numerical values and does not accept letters.

Click on the button and enter a number between 0 to 100 to get a grade. The grades are as follows; A > 79, B - 60 to 79, C - 59 to 49, D - 40 to 49, E - less than 40.
